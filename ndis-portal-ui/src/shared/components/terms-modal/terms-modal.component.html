Terms and Conditions

1. Purpose of the Portal

This application serves as an online platform that helps Participants and Coordinators manage NDIS-related service bookings.

  • Booking Support Only: The portal allows Participants to browse available services, submit booking requests, and track their booking status.
  • Coordinator Management: Coordinators may review bookings, update booking statuses, manage services, and monitor participant activity through the system.
  • No Professional Advice: Any information from the chatbot or service recommendation feature is for general guidance only. It should not be considered medical, legal, financial, clinical, or official NDIS plan advice.

2. Privacy and Data Use

We respect the privacy of user information and use collected data only for portal-related purposes.

  • Account and Booking Data: The system may store user details such as name, email, role, booking history, selected services, and submitted notes.
  • Chatbot Sessions: Chat history may be kept only during the active session to maintain conversation flow and may be cleared once the user logs out or the session ends.
  • Purpose of Collection: User data is used for account access, booking management, coordinator review, testing, and improving the user experience. User data will not be sold to third parties.

3. User Responsibilities and Security

Users are expected to use the portal responsibly and protect their accounts.

  • Account Security: Users must keep their login credentials private and must not allow others to use their account.
  • Accurate Information: Participants must provide correct booking details, including preferred dates, service selections, and notes when needed.
  • Prohibited Actions: Users must not attempt to access another user's data, bypass role restrictions, misuse the chatbot, upload harmful content, or interfere with the portal's security and functionality.

4. Booking and Service Terms

The portal helps manage booking requests, but submitting a request does not guarantee approval.

  • Pending Review: New booking requests are marked as Pending until reviewed by a Coordinator.
  • Status Updates: Coordinators may approve or cancel bookings based on service availability, booking details, or administrative review.
  • Service Availability: Services may be updated, activated, or deactivated by Coordinators. Participants are responsible for checking service information before submitting a booking.

5. Access Restriction and Limitation of Responsibility

Access to the portal may be restricted if a user violates these terms or misuses the system.

  • Account Restriction: The project team may suspend or limit access for users who provide false information, attempt unauthorized access, misuse portal features, or compromise user privacy and system security.
  • System Availability: The portal may experience interruptions due to testing, maintenance, technical issues, or third-party API problems.
  • Limitation of Responsibility: This portal is a training project. The project team is not responsible for decisions made based on incorrect user input, system errors, unavailable services, or AI-generated responses.
